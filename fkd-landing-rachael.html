The Work

This site exists on purpose, separate from Instagram and Facebook. Social platforms are useful, but they don't belong to the people who use them — algorithms decide who sees what, and accounts can vanish overnight.

This is a space the Delegation actually owns: a direct line to people who care about kimono in Florida, independent of any platform's rules.

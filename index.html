Florida, USA

Our purpose

To cultivate a vibrant, supportive community where the traditional art of wearing kimono is seamlessly and joyfully integrated into the unique lifestyle, climate, and culture of Florida. Florida Kimono strives to maintain the living directory, archive, and community network for Japanese culture in Florida by attempting to build a statewide network that includes both kimono sellers and enthusiasts, rather than a single local club, school, or business.

It exists for Florida-based kimono collectors, sellers, enthusiasts, educators, and cultural organizations who want a central place to connect, learn, preserve, and celebrate kimono culture.

Our mission

Our mission is to connect and celebrate the kimono community, support a vibrant culture and lifestyle, help members discover their style, and curate a trusted network to elevate every wardrobe and keep these traditions alive for the next generation.
